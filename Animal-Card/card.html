Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals in the world. It weighed about 24,000 pounds and was 13 feet tall at the shoulder! Elephants can live to be over 70 years old.

Length: 5.5-6 m
Lifespan: 60-70 yrs
Mass: 6000 kg
Height: 3.3 m

Elephants are large mammals of the family Elephantidae. Three species are currently recognized: the African bush elephant (Loxodonta africana), the African forest elephant (L. cyclotis), and the Asian elephant (Elephas maximus).
